# You can add cards and have their blanks formatted as desired when output by the bot.
# A simple blank (_) will be replaced by a white card with no changes. (_C) will Capitalize the first letter of the
# white card, typically used at the beginning of a sentence. (_T) will 'Titlecase the White Card', best used for turning
# a card into a movie, song, or book title. (_A) will make the white card ALL CAPS. (_S) will make the white card
# lowercasewithoutpunctuation, useful for turning them into website names. Have fun, and look below for examples!
